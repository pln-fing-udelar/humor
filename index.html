HUMOR

A Crowd-Annotated Spanish Corpus for Humor Analysis

Santiago Castro, Matías Cubero, Diego Garat and Guillermo Moncecchi.

Grupo de Procesamiento de Lenguaje Natural, Universidad de la República — Uruguay

HUMOR is a crowd-annotated corpus of almost 40k tweets, labeled by humor and funniness value.

Download

The dataset consists of the following 2 files:
  • Annotations CSV
  • Tweets CSV

It may be handy to work with an aggregated version of the Annotations file, which contains one row per tweet with the sum of annotations for each category: Annotations by Tweet CSV
